Brewery

The Wolf & Warrior is a new brewery and taproom located in the heart of White Plains NY. We offer up innovative and delicious beer brewed on-premises, top tier farm to table food, and the finest coffee available. We want to give our community a great place in which to kick back and chill, with great beer, food and friends.

About Us

I brewed my first beer (an IPA, naturally) back in Brooklyn in 2012, when the little wolf in the photo to the right was still getting ready to come into the world. 5 years later with a slew of wins in beer competitions all over the Northeast under my belt...and these beers, with MANY more to come, will soon be flowing from the taps at the Wolf & Warrior Brewing Company.
